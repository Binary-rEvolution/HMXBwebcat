Have you just published new results on a binary listed in the catalogue ? Did you read a paper with crucial information that we've missed ? Or have you discovered an entirely new HMXB in the Galaxy ?

If you want the information to be uploaded to the catalogue, please consider reaching us via our public github repository. There, you can submit a new "GitHub Issue" and provide details about your findings. Every field in the catalogue can be updated: identifiers, distances, orbital data... We are also interested in listing multi-wavelength counterparts to the HMXBs and their coordinates.

Your help is greatly appreciated !

-the Binary rEvolution team.
