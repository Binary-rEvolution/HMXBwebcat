Welcome to the online version of the Catalogue of High-Mass X-ray Binaries in the Galaxy: 5th Edition.


Use the navigation buttons to browse the catalogue, view update notes or download the catalogue in your preferred format.

This catalogue is meant to be updated as new information on HMXBs is published in the literature. If you wish to submit a suggestion or update a specific field in the catalogue, please visit the "Contributing" section.

We will issue updated downloadable versions regularly; in between major updates, any new piece of information will be stored in the "Notes" section and will also be available to the users within the browsable table in the "Catalogue" section.

If you wish to contact us directly, visit the "About" section.
